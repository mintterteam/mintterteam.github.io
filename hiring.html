Tech Stack

We are building an application, a protocol, and a decentralized network for content creation and publishing. We use libp2p and IPFS to build our protocol and decentralized network. We have significant inspirations from SSB, Dat, BitTorrent, and other P2P protocols. The backend is in Go. Frontend is in TypeScript with React. For the Editor, we are using Slate JS.
